# Tutorial: Building Your First Study

Welcome to the Study Builder tutorial. In this guide, you will learn how to create and configure a brand-new practice study from scratch. By following these steps, you will gain hands-on experience setting up the fundamental components needed to collect and manage clinical data.

## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have logged in to your environment with an account that has a Clinical Coordinator or Data Manager role.

## Step 1: Create a New Study

To begin, you will define the basic parameters of your study.

1. Navigate to the main dashboard.
2. Click on the **Build Study** menu option located in the top navigation bar.
3. Select **Create New Study** from the dropdown menu.
4. In the **Study Name** field, type a generic name such as "Cardiology Research Trial" (feel free to choose any name).
5. Leave the default settings for the remaining fields and click the **Save** button.

## Step 2: Define an Event

An Event represents a specific timepoint or visit where you collect data from a participant.

1. Once your study is created, navigate to the **Events** tab.
2. Click the **Add Event** button.
3. Enter "Baseline Visit" as the Event Name.
4. Under the Event Type dropdown, select **Scheduled**.
5. Click **Save Event**. You have now established a foundation for collecting data during a participant's first visit.

## Step 3: Add a Form

Forms (or Case Report Forms) are used to capture the actual data points.

1. Navigate to the **Forms** tab within your study builder interface.
2. Click on the **Create Form** button.
3. Provide a name for your form, such as "Vital Signs Assessment."
4. Use the drag-and-drop editor to add three simple fields: "Heart Rate," "Blood Pressure," and "Temperature."
5. Save your form by clicking the **Save** button at the bottom of the screen.

## Step 4: Link the Form to the Event

To ensure the system knows when to present your newly created form, you must assign it to the correct event.

1. Return to the **Events** tab and locate the "Baseline Visit" event you created earlier.
2. Click the **Edit** icon next to the event name.
3. In the **Associated Forms** section, click the **Add Form** button.
4. Check the box next to your "Vital Signs Assessment" form.
5. Click **Update Event** to confirm your changes.

## Conclusion

Congratulations! You have successfully created a practice study, defined a study event, created a data collection form, and linked them together. You can now use these skills to configure actual clinical trials within the platform.
